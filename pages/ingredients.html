Ingredient Glossary

Click any ingredient to learn more about its benefits and why we use it

Hydrocolloid

Medical-grade polymer that absorbs impurities and creates an optimal healing environment.

Key Benefits:

  • Draws out pus and fluid from blemishes
  • Creates moist healing environment
  • Prevents infection and scarring
  • FDA-approved for wound care

Hydrolyzed Collagen

Broken-down collagen molecules that penetrate deep to boost skin elasticity.

Key Benefits:

  • Improves skin elasticity by 28%
  • Reduces fine lines and wrinkles
  • Increases hydration retention
  • Clinically proven efficacy

Hyaluronic Acid

Super-hydrator that holds 1000x its weight in water for plump, dewy skin.

Key Benefits:

  • Intense hydration without greasiness
  • Plumps skin and smooths texture
  • Suitable for all skin types
  • Naturally found in skin

Red Light Therapy (630nm)

Specific wavelength that stimulates collagen production at the cellular level.

Key Benefits:

  • Boosts collagen by up to 31%
  • Reduces fine lines in 2 weeks
  • Improves skin texture and tone
  • NASA-researched technology

Niacinamide (Vitamin B3)

Multi-tasking vitamin that brightens, strengthens, and evens skin tone.

Key Benefits:

  • Reduces hyperpigmentation
  • Minimizes pore appearance
  • Strengthens skin barrier
  • Calms inflammation and redness

Peptides

Amino acid chains that signal skin to produce more collagen and elastin.

Key Benefits:

  • Stimulates collagen production
  • Firms and tightens skin
  • Reduces wrinkle depth
  • Repairs skin barrier
