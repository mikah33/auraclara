Step 1: Cleanse
Wash your face with a gentle cleanser and pat the area completely dry. The patch adheres best to clean, dry skin.

Step 2: Select Size
Choose a patch size that fully covers your blemish with a small border around it for maximum effectiveness.

Step 3: Apply
Peel the patch from the sheet and gently place it over the blemish. Press down on all edges to ensure proper adhesion.

Step 4: Leave On
Leave the patch on for at least 6 hours or overnight. The patch will turn white as it absorbs impurities. Remove gently and discard.

Pro Tip: For best results, apply to blemishes that have come to a head. You can wear patches under makeup during the day.
